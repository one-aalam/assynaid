A beautiful Jira assignee scanner with powerful features

Welcome to Assynaid!

Thank you for installing Assynaid, the modern and beautiful Jira assignee scanner. This extension will help you efficiently manage assignees on your Jira boards with powerful selection and grouping features.

One-Click Scanning

Quickly scan Jira boards to extract all assignees with a single click, saving you time and effort.

Group Management

Create and manage custom groups of assignees for better team organization and quick filtering.

Smart Selection

Select assignees in the extension and apply your selection directly to the Jira board with smooth animation.

How to Use Assynaid

  1. Navigate to any Jira board where you want to manage assignees.
  2. Click the Assynaid icon in your browser's toolbar to open the extension.
  3. Click "Scan Now" to extract all assignees from the current Jira board.
  4. Select assignees by clicking on them in the grid or list view.
  5. Create groups to organize assignees by team, department, or any other criteria.
  6. Apply your selection to the Jira board with a single click.
